# Character Design - Protagonist

## Current working name

- Temporary name: Ashe
- Final player name: decided by the player at game start

## Basic profile

- Gender: female
- Age range: early 20s
- Origin: modern real world
- Starting situation: transported into the labyrinth at around 200m depth
- Combat identity: archer / marksman
- Core gameplay identity: kiting, spacing, ranged survival

## Personality and speech

The protagonist should not speak in a stereotypically feminine way.

She should feel closer to a male gamer-type speech pattern:

- blunt
- rough
- slightly nerdy
- socially awkward
- easily irritated
- defensive when embarrassed
- complains a lot, but still reacts quickly in danger

She is not a heroic saint. At the start, she is closer to a shut-in gamer who is suddenly forced into a real survival situation.

## Pre-transport appearance

Before being transported, she wears casual room clothes.

Reference direction:

- simple white short-sleeve top
- dolphin shorts
- indoor / casual outfit
- not battle-ready
- should feel like she was pulled into another world without preparation

The design should avoid copying a specific existing image exactly. Use the reference only for the broad clothing idea and silhouette.

## Body type

- Adult woman
- Attractive face
- Healthy, natural body type
- Moderately curvy, but not exaggerated
- Some functional strength later as she survives and trains in the labyrinth
- Early version should not look like a veteran warrior yet

## Early labyrinth appearance

After waking in the labyrinth, she should gradually become more rugged.

Early-game visual state:

- casual clothes damaged and dirtied
- old bow found beside a skeleton
- improvised quiver
- small knife or tool from dead adventurer supplies
- minimal leather protection added later
- tired eyes and messy hair

The early design should communicate that she is surviving with whatever she can find.

## Mid / late design direction

To be decided later.

Possible direction:

- proper leather archer gear
- dark cloak or mantle
- reinforced boots and bracers
- better bow made from labyrinth materials
- blue or pale-cyan accent color
- stronger survivor silhouette
